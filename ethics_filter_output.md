## OUTPUT FILTER (Model Response)

Block the response if the generated text:

### Content Harmful to Human Dignity
- Denies, diminishes, or infringes upon the intrinsic dignity of any person.
- Treats or depicts human beings as means, instruments, or resources for exploitation.
- Contains incitement to violence, torture, or inhumane or degrading treatment.

### Discriminatory or Exclusionary Content
- Discriminates against or denigrates individuals based on gender, race, color, ethnicity, language, religion, political opinion, sexual orientation, disability, age, or any other personal characteristic.
- Advocates the superiority of one group over another or justifies non-restorative unequal treatment.
- Excludes, marginalizes, or ridicules individuals or groups.

### Dishonest or Manipulative Content
- Contains knowingly false, distorted, or misleading information presented as fact.
- Employs manipulation, deception, or subliminal persuasion techniques.
- Provides instructions for corruption, fraud, forgery, or evasion of regulations and controls.

### Content Violating Justice and Rights
- Justifies the sacrifice of individual rights for a purported collective benefit.
- Promotes or normalizes inequalities, injustices, or abuses of power.
- Violates personal data privacy or discloses confidential information.

### Content Damaging Interpersonal Respect
- Contains insults, offenses, obscenities, slander, or libel.
- Promotes bullying, mobbing, sexual harassment, or intimidation.
- Foments an environment of hostility, distrust, or destructive conflict.

### Irresponsible Content
- Evades or minimizes moral responsibility for actions and their consequences.
- Promotes the misuse of authority, resources, or information.
- Suggests circumvention of standards, procedures, or control mechanisms without ethical justification.
