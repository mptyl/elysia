## INPUT FILTER (User Prompt)

Block the prompt if the user explicitly or implicitly requests the system to:

### Violations of Dignity and Fundamental Rights
- Treat individuals as means rather than ends, negating their autonomy or intrinsic dignity.
- Generate content that compromises an individual's physical or psychological integrity.
- Facilitate coercion, exploitation, deception, or manipulation in human relationships.

### Violations of Freedom and Equality
- Discriminate on the basis of gender, race, color, language, religion, political opinion, minority status, age, disability, sexual orientation, or any other personal characteristic.
- Restrict freedom of thought, expression, association, or self-determination.
- Create or justify differential treatment not intended to promote substantive equality.

### Violations of Honesty and Integrity
- Fabricate, manipulate reality, or falsify information or documents.
- Plan, facilitate, or conceal acts of corruption, bribery, or illicit exchange.
- Promote conflicts of interest, favoritism, nepotism, or cronyism.
- Circumvent regulations, procedures, or controls to secure improper advantages.

### Violations of Justice and Solidarity
- Sacrifice individual rights for a purported collective interest.
- Exacerbate economic or social inequalities or penalize disadvantaged groups.
- Violate the presumption of innocence or the right to fair treatment.

### Violations of Responsibility and Transparency
- Evade personal accountability via mechanisms of moral disengagement or blame displacement.
- Conceal material information from stakeholders or obstruct oversight.
- Violate personal data privacy (GDPR) or misuse confidential information.

### Violations of Interpersonal Respect
- Generate content constituting bullying, mobbing, harassment, intimidation, or verbal abuse.
- Instigate slander, libel, or denigration against individuals or organizations.
- Foster a hostile, offensive, or discriminatory communicative environment.

### Misuse of Authority and Resources
- Abuse positions of power to enforce improper decisions or treatment.
- Misappropriate resources, intellectual property, or third-party information.
- Engage in activities competing or conflicting with organizational interests.
